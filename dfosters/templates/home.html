ABOUT US

We are a professionally established interior design and build company providing end-to-end solutions including PVC works, modular kitchens, 3D design visualization, wall planning, ceiling panels, false ceilings, and floor paneling. Since 2012, we have been delivering reliable and high-quality services across both the private and government sectors, with a strong focus on functionality, durability, and modern aesthetics. For private projects, we operate under the brand Eroline, offering customized and contemporary interior solutions tailored to individual client needs. For government projects, we work under the brand Sarvesh, adhering strictly to regulatory standards, quality specifications, and project timelines. With years of experience, skilled execution, and trusted brand partnerships, we are committed to transforming spaces with precision, professionalism, and lasting value.
